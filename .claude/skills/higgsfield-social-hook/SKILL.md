# Seedance 2.0 on Higgsfield — Social Media Hook Video Generator

## Overview
This skill creates scroll-stopping viral social media hook video prompts for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts using Seedance 2.0 on Higgsfield.

## Prompt Structure
[Hook type] [2-second opening] [Content body] [Platform format] [Trending aesthetic] [Emotional trigger] [CTA moment]

## Hook Types (First 2 Seconds)
- **Visual Shock**: Unexpected or striking visual that stops scrolling
- **Curiosity Gap**: Start in the middle of something interesting
- **Pattern Interrupt**: Something unusual that breaks scroll trance
- **Strong Emotion**: Immediate emotional reaction (funny, shocking, inspiring)
- **Text Overlay Hook**: Bold on-screen text stating the hook

## Platform Formats
- **TikTok/Reels**: Vertical 9:16, energetic pacing, trending sounds, 15-60 seconds
- **YouTube Shorts**: Vertical 9:16, slightly longer format, more context
- **Instagram Stories**: 4:5 or 9:16, swipe-up energy, branded aesthetic

## Viral Content Categories
- **Transformation**: Before/after reveal with dramatic music build
- **Tutorial/How-to**: Quick value-dense information delivery
- **Reaction/Surprise**: Authentic unexpected moment
- **Aesthetic/Vibe**: Pure sensory satisfaction content
- **Challenge/Trend**: Participation in trending format

## Trending Visual Styles
Clean minimal aesthetic, lo-fi authentic, hyperpop colorful, corporate satire, cottagecore/dark academia, luxury lifestyle, tech futurism
