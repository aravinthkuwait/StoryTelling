# Seedance 2.0 on Higgsfield — Comic-to-Video Converter

## Overview
This skill animates static comic book panels, manga pages, webtoons, and illustrated storyboards into dynamic video using Seedance 2.0 on Higgsfield.

## Prompt Structure
[Source art style] [Panel/page description] [Animation type] [Motion effects] [Panel transitions] [Speed/timing] [Audio mood]

## Comic Art Styles
- **Western Comics**: "Marvel/DC superhero style", "indie comic book", "underground comix"
- **Manga**: "shounen manga", "shojo style", "seinen dark manga", "chibi manga"
- **Webtoon**: "Korean webtoon", "vertical scroll format", "clean digital art"
- **Graphic Novel**: "painterly graphic novel", "noir illustrated", "European bande dessinée"

## Animation Techniques
- **Ken Burns Effect**: Slow pan and zoom across static panels
- **Parallax**: Foreground/background layers moving at different speeds
- **Motion Comics**: Characters subtly animated within panels
- **Panel Transitions**: Cinematic cuts, wipes, fades between panels

## Motion Effects for Comics
- Speed lines animating into frame
- Impact flashes and hit effects
- Speech bubble pop-ins
- Dramatic zoom into key panels
- Camera drift across wide establishing shots
