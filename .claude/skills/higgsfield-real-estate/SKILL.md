# Seedance 2.0 on Higgsfield — Real Estate & Architecture Video Generator

## Overview
This skill creates real estate, architecture, and interior design showcase video prompts for Seedance 2.0 on Higgsfield. Covers property listings, architectural reveals, interior tours, and construction showcases.

## Prompt Structure
[Property type] [Key feature to showcase] [Camera movement] [Lighting time of day] [Target buyer emotion] [Visual treatment] [Tour style]

## Property Types
- **Luxury Residential**: High-end homes, penthouses, villas, estates
- **Commercial**: Office spaces, retail, hospitality, mixed-use
- **Interior Design**: Staged homes, model units, design showcases
- **Architecture**: New construction, architectural landmarks, modern design
- **Development**: Under construction, site reveals, before/after

## Camera Movement Styles
- **Drone Aerial**: Exterior approach, neighborhood context, roof reveal
- **Interior Walk-through**: Smooth dolly through rooms
- **Room Reveal**: Push into room as light comes on
- **360 Panorama**: Standing point capturing full space
- **Window View**: From inside looking out to surroundings

## Lighting Approaches
- **Golden Hour**: Warm exterior, inviting atmosphere
- **Blue Hour**: Twilight with interior lights glowing
- **Bright Day**: Maximum natural light, open and airy
- **Dramatic Interior**: Moody accent lighting, luxury feel
- **Before/After**: Construction to completion lighting

## Buyer Emotion Targets
- "Imagine living here" lifestyle aspiration
- Investment/value proposition
- Family home warmth and safety
- Modern urban professional prestige
- Luxury and exclusivity
