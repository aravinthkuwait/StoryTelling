# Seedance 2.0 on Higgsfield — E-Commerce Ad Generator

## Overview
This skill creates compelling e-commerce product advertisement prompts for Seedance 2.0 on Higgsfield. Covers all product categories: fashion, beauty, food, electronics, home goods, and more.

## Prompt Structure
[Product type] [Key selling point] [Visual treatment] [Customer emotion] [Platform/format] [CTA] [Brand aesthetic]

## Product Category Approaches
- **Fashion**: "lifestyle model wearing product", "outfit in motion", "fabric texture detail"
- **Beauty/Skincare**: "product glamour shot", "before/after reveal", "texture close-up"
- **Food/Beverage**: "appetite appeal close-up", "preparation reveal", "indulgent slow-motion"
- **Electronics/Tech**: "feature demonstration", "lifestyle usage shot", "unboxing reveal"
- **Home Goods**: "lifestyle home setting", "problem/solution reveal", "before/after transformation"

## Visual Treatments
- Hero product shot with dramatic lighting
- Lifestyle scene with product in natural use
- Flatlay overhead with supporting props
- Detail macro revealing quality/texture
- Comparison/transformation reveal

## Platform-Specific Formats
- **TikTok/Reels**: Vertical 9:16, hook in first 2 seconds, trending audio vibe
- **Instagram Feed**: Square or 4:5, polished aesthetic, aspirational mood
- **YouTube Pre-roll**: 16:9, CTA before 5 seconds, longer story arc
- **Amazon/Shopify**: Clean white background, feature highlights, multiple angles
