# Seedance 2.0 on Higgsfield — Fashion Lookbook Video Generator

## Overview
This skill creates fashion lookbook, model showcase, and style video prompts for Seedance 2.0 on Higgsfield. Covers editorial fashion, streetwear, runway, and commercial fashion campaigns.

## Prompt Structure
[Fashion category] [Model/subject] [Outfit description] [Movement style] [Setting/location] [Lighting] [Camera style] [Mood/aesthetic]

## Fashion Categories
- **Editorial**: High fashion, avant-garde, artistic expression
- **Commercial**: Accessible fashion, aspirational lifestyle
- **Streetwear**: Urban, casual, youth culture, authenticity
- **Luxury**: Premium materials, refined elegance, aspirational
- **Athleisure**: Active, healthy lifestyle, movement-focused
- **Runway**: Dramatic theatrical presentation, catwalk energy

## Model Movement Styles
- Confident catwalk strut
- Natural lifestyle movement
- Dynamic jumping/spinning transitions
- Slow-motion fabric flow reveal
- Multiple outfit quick-change cuts

## Location/Setting Archetypes
- White studio (clean, focus on clothing)
- Urban architecture (contrast, character)
- Natural landscape (organic, editorial)
- Luxury interior (aspirational lifestyle)
- Street/city (authentic, relatable)

## Lighting for Fashion
- Golden hour warmth (romantic, soft)
- Studio strobe (sharp, editorial)
- Natural window light (authentic, soft)
- Dramatic backlighting (silhouette, dramatic)
- Neon/color gel (editorial, mood)
