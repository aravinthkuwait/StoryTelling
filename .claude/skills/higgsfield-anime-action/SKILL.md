- Speed lines (action), sakura petals (drama/romance)
- Power aura effects (energy glow around character)

- Dynamic anime opening sequence style

- **Shoujo**: Pastels, flowers, sparkles, soft gradients
- **Dark/Seinen**: Muted tones, shadows, dramatic highlights
- **Mecha**: Metallic blues, grays, orange/red accents
